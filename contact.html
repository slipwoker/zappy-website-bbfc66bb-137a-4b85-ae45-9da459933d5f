    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is your typical project timeline?

    Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A standard hotel photography project typically takes 2-3 days for shooting and 1-2 weeks for post-production. Rush services are available for time-sensitive projects.

    Do you travel for projects?

    Yes, I work with properties worldwide. Travel expenses are included in project quotes for locations outside my local area. I'm experienced in coordinating logistics for international projects.

    What deliverables are included?

    All projects include high-resolution edited images optimized for both print and digital use. You'll receive images in multiple formats suitable for websites, social media, print materials, and other marketing channels.

    How far in advance should I book?

    I recommend booking 4-6 weeks in advance for optimal scheduling, though I can accommodate shorter timelines when availability permits. Peak seasons may require earlier booking.
